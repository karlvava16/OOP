The increasing rate of digital transformation across the AsiaPacific region has created heaps of data ranging from online customer transactions to footfall traffic, making data analytics more critical than ever to leverage all that data. In the healthcare industry, a large amount of data is already being collected to generate insights on emerging conditions and to improve patient care. Yet, like other industries, the healthcare sector continues to grapple with issues such as data silos and security challenges that stand in the way of harnessing insights.
